Popular Destinations

New York

The views of the New York skyline from the Brooklyn Bridge include views of Lower Manhattan and the World Trade Center and even the Statue of Liberty. If you look opposite of Lady Liberty you’ll also get great views of the Manhattan Bridge.

Paris

The Eiffel Tower is a famous iron monument in Paris, France that's a must-see for tourists. It's the world's most visited paid monument, with 5,889,000 visitors in 2022, and is considered one of the 7 wonders of the world by UNESCO.

Tokyo

Tokyo’s most visited temple enshrines a golden image of Kannon (the Buddhist goddess of mercy), which, according to legend, was miraculously pulled out of the nearby Sumida-gawa by two fishermen in AD 628. The image has remained on the spot ever since but is never on public display. The present structure dates from 1958.

Featured Activities

Hiking the Grand Canyon

At 18 miles long and more than 6,000 feet deep, the Grand Canyon exhibits 2 billion years of geology along the Colorado River. When you hike down into the canyon, you get to see those red, rust, rose, white, and burnt orange rock sequences up close. Arizona’s dry air preserves these colorful rocks well.

Whale Watching in Alaska

Whale watching in Alaska is best during the summer months, from May to September, when whales migrate from tropical Pacific waters to Alaska's coastal waters. Most popular whale watching destinations include: Kenai Fjords National Park and Resurrection Bay and the Kodiak area,Bering Sea etc

Snorkeling in Hawaii

Hawaii has many snorkeling destinations, including marine life conservation areas, underwater parks, and other top spots:Hanauma Bay,Kealakekua Bay,Turtle Town,Molokini,Turtle Canyon,Honolua Bay,Manta ray night dive. snorkeling Hawaii spots with excellent reef depths of 15 feet or less.
